Kezia Santoso 蔡丽霞

CIO - Writer

Student by day, superhero by night. Balancing academic responsibilities with my duty as a matchmaker isn't easy. But no matter how challenging it gets, I will never give up my responsibility of connecting all the single people around the world. Still, I also dream of finding someone to call my own. I don't know if I can go on being all alone any longer. As they say, you either die a hero or live long enough to see yourself become the villain.
